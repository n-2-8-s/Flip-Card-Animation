Zayn Malik

Singer

Zain Javadd Malik, known professionally as Zayn Malik or simply Zayn, is a British singer. Malik auditioned as a solo contestant for the British music competition television series The X Factor in 2010.

Born: January 12, 1993 (age 30 years), Bradford, United Kingdom
Parents: Yaser Malik, Trisha Malik
Siblings: Safaa Malik, Waliyha Malik, Doniya Malik
Height: 1.75 m
Nationality: English
